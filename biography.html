Daniel Ricciardo - Full Biography

Daniel Ricciardo, born July 1, 1989, in Perth, Australia, is one of the most charismatic drivers in Formula 1. Raised in a racing family, Daniel started karting at a young age and soon showed promise in the world of motorsports.

Daniel in his early karting days.

In 2008, Daniel made the leap to European racing, competing in Formula Renault 2.0. His breakthrough moment came when he won the Formula Renault Western European Cup, marking his entry into the radar of Formula 1 teams.

Daniel celebrating his first F1 victory at the 2014 Canadian Grand Prix.

In 2014, Daniel joined Red Bull Racing and quickly made an impact by securing his first F1 win in Canada. That season, he went on to claim two more victories and established himself as a top driver in the sport.

Career Highlights

2011: Formula 1 Debut

Daniel Ricciardo made his Formula 1 debut with the HRT team at the 2011 British Grand Prix. His first race marked the beginning of an exciting journey in the world of motorsport.

2011: Scuderia Toro Rosso

After a year as a test driver for Red Bull Racing, Ricciardo was promoted to the Toro Rosso team, where he continued to develop his skills and gain valuable experience.

2014: Red Bull Racing

Ricciardo joined Red Bull Racing and had a stellar season, winning three races and finishing third in the drivers' championship. He became the first Australian to win a race for Red Bull.

2014: Canadian Grand Prix Victory

Daniel achieved his first Grand Prix victory at the 2014 Canadian Grand Prix, showcasing his exceptional driving skills and race strategy in one of the most prestigious races on the calendar.

2018: First Race with Renault

After four successful years with Red Bull, Ricciardo made the move to Renault, where he aimed to help the team return to competitiveness in Formula 1.

2021: Italian Grand Prix Win

Ricciardo secured his first podium finish with McLaren at the 2021 Italian Grand Prix, marking his first win in two years.

2021: McLaren Signing

In 2021 Ricciardo signed with McLaren, bringing his experience and talent to the team. He made an immediate impact with impressive performances and a notable win at the 2021 Italian Grand Prix.

2023: Strong Performances

Daniel returned to the grid with AlphaTauri in 2023, demonstrating his skill and determination to reclaim his place among the top drivers in Formula 1.

2024: A Sad End

As of September 2024, Ricciardo was released by VCARB and replaced by Liam Lawson. After an amazing career and a lot of big smiles, it seems like the career of the Honey Badger is sadly over.
